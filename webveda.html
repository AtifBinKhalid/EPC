FrontEnd webVeda Hackathon 2021

About Event

There will be two rounds in this event.
1st Round will be Quiz Round, which is based on fundamentals of HTML, CSS, & JavaScript. Students can participate either individually or in group (not more than 3 members). Quiz will take place on D2C platform in which 30 MCQâ€™s will be given and each question will be of 5 marks and this round will be of 20 minutes.

Top performers will be selected for final round i.e. frontEnd webVeda Hackathon. Final Round: Selected students/teams can participate in final round.

In final round, we will provide general web design and participants have to develop that design using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Along with HTML, CSS, JS file, participants also have to create a Readme.txt file which includes their details (name, registered email Id, branch, course, year) and Team name. And then convert it into zip file (zip name: Team name).

Note: Use of Bootstrap or any other framework/library are strictly prohibited and lead to disqualification.

Judgement will be on the basis of:
- Responsiveness of web page
- Creativity
- Loading time
- Clean Code

Total Time: 1st round: 30 minutes
Final round: 5 hours + 30 minutes for submission.

Cash Prizes:
1st Prize: 1000 Rupees
2nd Prize: 700 Rupees
3rd Prize: 500 Rupees
